Our Aim

Pace Courier Service aims to serve its customers by providing the best delivery service.

As industry developed in Pakistan, and more raw materials and other products were to be dispatched all over Pakistan, the need for a courier service grew, and Pace Courier Service found itself fulfilling a more significant need-helping local industry grow. Pace Courier Service employees have been trained and placed to help clients find services that best fulfill their needs.

We have the largest network in terms of destinations, reaching remote Pakistani areas that no other courier service reaches.

The distribution network is designed in such a way so that mail from all over Pakistan is distributed the very next day except for a few areas/stations mentioned in our Station List, where local underdevelopment of resources has not made this possible, and where many courier services do not offer delivery. However, our dream is to connect all Pakistanis, whether they live in under-developed villages or large metropolitan cities, and we deliver everywhere.
